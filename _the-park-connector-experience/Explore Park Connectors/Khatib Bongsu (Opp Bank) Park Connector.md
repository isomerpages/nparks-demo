## Khatib Bongsu (Opp Bank) Park Connector

Starting from the Yishun Ave 8, Khatib Bongsu (Opp Bank) Park Connector runs along Sungei Khatib Bongsu towards Yishun Pond Park. It ends at Yishun Central.
