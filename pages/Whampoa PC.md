Starting from Toa Payoh Town Park, Whampoa Park Connector runs along Sungei Whampoa and joins up with Kallang Park Connector at Bendemeer Road. It contains two PUB Active, Beautiful, Clean Water programme features on both banks of the Kallang River – River Vista and Kolam Ayer Waterfront. The park connector takes you through Jalan Rajah, Kim Keat, Whampoa, St George’s and Bendemeer residential estates. You can also enjoy a wide range of food outlets along Balestier Road, with some operating around the clock.>
